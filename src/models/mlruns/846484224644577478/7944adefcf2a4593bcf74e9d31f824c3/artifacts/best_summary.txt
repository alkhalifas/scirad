The scientific articles reviewed focus on the integration of agent-based frameworks and artificial intelligence (AI) models in various domains, emphasizing methodologies, key findings, and implications.

1. **Leveraging Large Language Models and Agent-Based Systems for Scientific Data Analysis**: This study introduces the LITURAt system, which employs an agent-based architecture to enhance large language models (LLMs) for scientific data analysis. By incorporating external data retrieval and analysis tools, LITURAt addresses challenges related to factual accuracy and domain-specific precision in LLMs. The system's architecture ensures context-aware outputs, making scientific information more accessible to a broad audience, including nonexperts. This approach highlights the potential of agent-based frameworks in improving the reliability and applicability of AI in scientific domains.

2. **Measuring Water Pollution Effects on Antimicrobial Resistance through Explainable AI**: This research utilizes explainable AI within the One Health framework to analyze the impact of water pollution on antimicrobial resistance. By employing a comprehensive set of indicators, the study predicts mortality related to several pathogens. The findings underscore the critical role of water quality and accessibility in influencing antimicrobial resistance outcomes, suggesting that AI-driven insights can inform decision-making and monitoring efforts in public health.

3. **Smart Pharmaceutical Monitoring System for Diabetes Management**: The development of a smart pharmaceutical monitoring system for diabetes patients is explored, leveraging AI to create personalized medication schedules and self-management programs. This system aims to improve treatment adherence and efficacy by addressing challenges such as treatment complexity and polypharmacy. The study emphasizes the transformative potential of AI in enhancing healthcare delivery and patient outcomes in chronic disease management.

Overall, these articles demonstrate the diverse applications of agent-based frameworks and AI in improving data analysis, healthcare, and public health outcomes. The integration of AI technologies facilitates more precise, context-aware, and accessible solutions across various fields, highlighting the importance of continued research and development in these areas.
