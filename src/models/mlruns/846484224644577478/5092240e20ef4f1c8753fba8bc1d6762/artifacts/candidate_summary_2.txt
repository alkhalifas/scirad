The reviewed scientific articles explore various applications of agent and multiagent-based frameworks in artificial intelligence (AI) models and systems, emphasizing their methodologies, key findings, and implications in diverse fields.

The article "Leveraging Large Language Models and Agent-Based Systems for Scientific Data Analysis: Validation Study" introduces the LITURAt system, which integrates large language models with an agent-based architecture to enhance the analysis of scientific data. This framework addresses issues of factual accuracy and domain-specific precision by incorporating external data retrieval tools, ensuring context-aware outputs. The study highlights the potential of such systems to make scientific information more accessible to both experts and nonexperts, suggesting significant implications for scientific communication and data analysis.

In "Measuring water pollution effects on antimicrobial resistance through explainable artificial intelligence," the researchers employ an explainable AI approach within the One Health framework to study antimicrobial resistance. By analyzing various indicators, they identify water accessibility and quality as critical factors in antimicrobial resistance-related mortality. This study underscores the importance of AI in environmental health research, providing insights for decision support and monitoring.

The "Smart Pharmaceutical Monitoring System With Personalized Medication Schedules and Self-Management Programs for Patients With Diabetes" article discusses the integration of AI and digital technologies into diabetes management. The system aims to enhance treatment adherence through personalized medication schedules and self-management programs, addressing the complexities of pharmacological therapy in diabetes care. This study highlights the transformative potential of AI in personalizing healthcare and improving patient outcomes.

Collectively, these articles demonstrate the versatility and impact of agent and multiagent-based frameworks in AI, offering innovative solutions across scientific data analysis, healthcare, and environmental research. They emphasize the critical role of AI in enhancing precision, accessibility, and decision-making in complex domains.
