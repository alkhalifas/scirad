The articles reviewed agent and multiagent based frameworks for artificial intelligence models and systems. They found that these frameworks are effective in improving the performance and scalability of AI systems by allowing for decentralized decision-making and collaboration among agents. The methodologies employed in these studies included developing algorithms for agent communication, coordination, and learning. The implications of these findings suggest that agent-based frameworks can enhance the capabilities of AI systems in various applications such as robotics, healthcare, and finance. Overall, the research highlights the potential of agent and multiagent systems in advancing artificial intelligence technology.
