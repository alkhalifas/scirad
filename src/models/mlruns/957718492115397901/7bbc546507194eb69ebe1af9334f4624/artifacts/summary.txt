The articles reviewed agent and multiagent based frameworks for artificial intelligence models and systems, highlighting the importance of these frameworks in enhancing the capabilities of AI systems. The key findings of the articles suggest that agent-based models can effectively simulate complex systems by breaking them down into individual agents with specific behaviors and interactions. The methodologies employed in these studies involved designing and implementing agent-based frameworks to test and validate AI models in various scenarios. The implications of these findings indicate that agent-based frameworks can improve the efficiency, scalability, and adaptability of AI systems, leading to more robust and intelligent applications in various fields. Overall, the articles underscore the significance of agent-based approaches in advancing artificial intelligence research and development.
